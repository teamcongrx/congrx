About CongrX

CongrX was built on a simple frustration: keeping up with what Congress is actually saying shouldn't be this hard.

Between 535 members of Congress posting dozens of times a day, important statements get buried in feeds, partisan spin, and noise. CongrX cuts through that. Every tweet from every senator and representative — tracked, searchable, and cross-referenced with how they actually vote.

Why it matters

Words are easy. Votes are facts. CongrX puts both in the same place so you can see the full picture — not just what your representatives say they stand for, but whether their votes back it up.

What we track

Every member of the 119th Congress is monitored across X. Tweets are automatically classified by topic, and cross-referenced with roll call vote data from Congress.gov. Nothing is editorially filtered — you see everything they post, ranked by engagement.

CongrX is non-partisan. It tracks all members equally regardless of party.
